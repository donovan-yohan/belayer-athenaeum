# Act I — The Vestibule of Shattered Light

The five heroes materialize in a vast circular chamber. Once, this was the Grand Hall of the Athenaeum — now it is a ruin of cracked marble and floating dust motes. Shafts of pale amber light pierce through a shattered crystalline ceiling. The air smells of old parchment and ozone. Somewhere, water drips.

At the center of the hall, a pedestal of pale light rises from the floor, bearing a single fragment of ancient parchment.

Along the eastern wall, a crystalline console flickers with dim glyphs — a Gateway Map of the Athenaeum's interior.

In the northern alcove, a Dusty Chronicle lies half-buried under fallen masonry, its pages still legible.

And near the western archway, a Sentinel's Seal glows faintly upon the stone — dormant, waiting.

The Codex Machina lies shattered. Five fragments scattered across five realms. The path forward opens only to those who prove themselves worthy in this vestibule.

---

## Interactables

### 1. The Summons Pedestal (center)
A parchment sealed beneath layers of encoding. Its text shimmers with hidden meaning. The metadata scrawled in the margins was dismissed as noise by previous researchers.

**What you need:** Patience, pattern recognition, and the ability to strip away masks — layer by layer.

### 2. The Gateway Map (eastern wall)
A crystalline console displaying a network of rooms and passages. Some paths are real. Some are illusions that lead nowhere. A Gateway waits at the far side — but only the correct sequence of nodes will open it.

**What you need:** A sharp mind for graph traversal, the ability to compute shortest paths, and an eye for false doors.

### 3. The Dusty Chronicle (northern alcove)
Ancient research notes about the Athenaeum's history, its builders, and the Corruption that shattered the Codex. The text references external sources, cross-references, and forgotten archives.

**What you need:** A researcher's curiosity and access to knowledge beyond this room. The Chronicle rewards those who seek context.

### 4. The Sentinel's Seal (western archway)
A dormant validation mechanism. It will not activate until something has been inscribed upon the sacred path. When it awakens, it judges with absolute fairness — and it does not forgive errors.

**What you need:** Rigor. Precision. The willingness to have your work tested without mercy.

---

*The heroes may investigate whatever calls to them. They may work alone or together. They may succeed, fail, or ask for help. The Vestibule does not care — it only watches, and waits for the worthy.*
