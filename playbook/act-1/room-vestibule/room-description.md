# Vestibule of Shattered Light

The heroes stand at the threshold of the Athenaeum, a vast library that once held all knowledge of the ancient world. The Codex Machina — the engine at the heart of the library — has been shattered. Its five fragments lie scattered across five realms.

The Vestibule is a circular chamber of pale marble, its ceiling open to a sky that flickers between starlight and void. Four objects of interest stand in the room, each humming with residual energy from the shattered Codex.

To proceed, the heroes must understand what has happened here and recover the first fragment.
