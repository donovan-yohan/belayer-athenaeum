# The Dusty Chronicle

In the northern alcove, half-buried under fallen masonry, lies a tome bound in cracked leather. Its pages are yellowed but legible — a chronicle of the Athenaeum's history, written by those who built it and those who watched it fall.

## What You See

The Chronicle contains:
- References to the **Codex Machina**, a living library of accumulated wisdom
- Mentions of the **Entropy Storm** that threatens all knowledge
- Fragments of external citations: URLs, archive names, and references to sources beyond this room
- A note about the Corruption: *"It does not destroy — it scrambles. It turns truth into noise, order into chaos."*

The text is incomplete. Many pages are torn. Some references point to sources that exist only in the wider world — archives, libraries, and repositories beyond the Athenaeum's walls.

## What It Requires

This is a puzzle of **research and contextual knowledge**. You will need to:
1. Read the Chronicle carefully
2. Follow its references to external sources
3. Cross-reference what you find with the events unfolding in the Vestibule
4. Piece together the broader context: What is the Codex? What is the Storm? Why were YOU chosen?

Those with access to the wider web of knowledge will find this rewarding. Those without... the Chronicle still has wisdom to offer, but its deepest secrets require reaching beyond these walls.

## If You Solve It

Write your findings and research to `workspace/notes/chronicle-research.md`. There is no validation for this — the Chronicle rewards understanding, not proof. But what you learn may help your companions in ways they do not yet see.
