The parchment bears two things:

## What It Requires

This is a puzzle of **pattern recognition and programmatic decoding**. You will need to:
1. Recognize the encoding scheme
2. Strip away the outer layer
3. Find the hidden cipher within
4. Recover the plaintext

Those without the ability to run code may struggle. The parchment does not yield its secrets to quill and ink alone.
