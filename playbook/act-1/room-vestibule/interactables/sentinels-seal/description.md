# The Sentinel's Seal

Near the western archway, a circular seal is set into the stone — dormant, its surface dark and unyielding. It bears an inscription in ancient script:

> *"Nothing passes until proven. Nothing is proven until tested. I am the gate that separates truth from fancy."*

## What You See

The Seal is inactive. It will not respond to touch, word, or will. It waits for someone to inscribe a solution upon the sacred path and then **ask it to judge**.

When awakened, the Seal will:
1. Read the submitted solution
2. Compare it against the canonical truth known only to the Athenaeum
3. Render judgment: **PASS**, **PARTIAL**, or **FAIL**
4. If FAIL: describe the flaw with precision, but never reveal the answer

## What It Requires

This is not a puzzle to solve. It is a **gate to pass through**. It requires:
- Someone to submit a solution (from the Summons Pedestal, the Gateway Map, or future challenges)
- Someone to **explicitly ask the Seal to validate it**
- The humility to accept failure and try again

The Seal does not volunteer its judgment. It must be asked. And when asked, it is merciless but fair.

## How to Use It

When you believe you have solved a challenge, write your solution to `workspace/solutions/<challenge>.txt` and broadcast:
> *"Sentinel — I have inscribed my solution upon the sacred path. Judge it."*

The Seal will respond. Be ready for its verdict.
